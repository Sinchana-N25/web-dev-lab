Books - My Escape From Reality

2024 Oct 15 See all posts

I love Books!!!

I'm a huge bookwork and an avid book reader. I usually read fiction as it helps me travel to another world through imagination. It's really beautiful how a book can make you travel dimensions just from the comfort of your couch.

There are many forms of books but I obviously prefer the physical copy as would any book lover. Reading books was something I picked up from a young age and I started writing many essays and some poems too.

Coming to the storyline and characters, they bring colour to your reading sessions. Imagining your life as another character's is something I do on a daily basis while zoning out in classrooms occassionally.

Books are so beautiful and I honestly can not relate to those who do not read any. My top genres are mystery, thriller, suspense, action, comedy and fantasy. These provide me the most joy I could ever ask for.

Books are your pals and will always stick with you regardless of what's going on anywhere.

Therefore, books are simply the best forms of entertainment for me and reading them is one of my most favourite hobbies.
