The Power of Placebo

2024 Nov 26 See all posts

It is a phenomenon where drugs, treatments and therapies that aren’t supposed to have an effect and often fake make people feel better.

The phrase placebo is derived from the Latin phrase “I shall please” In 1996,56 volunteers took part in a study to test a new pain-killer called “Trivaricaine.”

On every volunteer one index finger was covered in a new pain-killer while the other remained untouched. Then both were squeezed in painful clamps. The volunteers reported that the treated finger hurt less than the untreated one. The twist is Trivaricaine wasn’t even a pain-killer it was a fake concotion with no pain easing property at all. The volunteers felt that the pain-killer worked even though it was fake was because of the placebo effect.Placebo had to be identical to real treatments in order to be convincing, hence they took the form of sugar pills, water-filled injections and shame surgeries(fake surgeries).

The active ingredient is expectation, they work because we expect them to. Thanks to the placebo effect, patience have experienced relief from range of ailments even though all they received was a fake drug or shame surgery. Researchers think that if a person believes a fake treatment is real, their expectations of recovery actually do trigger physiological factors that improve their symptoms.

Placebo’s seem to be capable of causing measurable change in blood pressure, heart rate and the release of pain-reducing chemicals like endorphins. Placebo’s even reduce levels of stress hormones like adrenaline, which can slow the harmful effects of an ailment.

The placebo eff ect even has it’s cons. If somebody believes a fake treatment has cured them, they may miss out on drugs or therapies that are proven to work. The positive effect may fade over time and they often do fade. Placebo’s also have an influence on clinical results which motivate scientists to discover how it yields such power.

Despite the fact that we know a lot about the human body, there are still some strange and enduring mysteries like the placebo effect.
